About Me

Hi, I am Jason Castromero, a current film student who specializes in video editing. In fact, I love nearly anything and every film-related, from: movies, to TV shows, to animated shorts. I've always been impassioned becoming an editor myself; creating videos in a recreational sense for over 6 years to date- with the dream of becoming a professional film editor as my end goal.

In the meantime, I do run a Youtube gaming channel based around the popularized video-game League of Legends as: a leisure activity, to create a film portfolio without the necessary equipment, and to practice and improve my skills as an editor.
